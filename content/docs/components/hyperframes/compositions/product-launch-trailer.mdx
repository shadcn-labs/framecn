Add the composition to your host HTML file:

```html

  data-composition-id="product-launch-trailer"
  data-composition-src="compositions/product-launch-trailer.html"
  data-start="0"
  data-duration="8"
  data-track-index="1"
  data-width="1920"
  data-height="1080"
```
